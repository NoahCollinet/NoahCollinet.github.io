My work focuses on using methods from artificial intelligence to perform certain tasks in data mining. The integration of specific reasoning approaches (SAT, CSP, ASP, preferences) from artificial intelligence for the improvement of extraction processes constitutes an important research problem. The goal of this work is the integration of preference-based argumentation in data mining in order to address two problems, namely explicability and inconsistency handling.
